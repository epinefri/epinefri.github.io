Average travel speeds and duration by train and car from Bucharest to Romania counties in Tableau

2022-11-06

Introduction

Comparison of trip duration between Bucharest and county seats of Romania, by train versus by car.

Data sources:

  1. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Counties_of_Romania
    • Counties and county seats
    • NUTS code (needed for Tableau maps)
  2. https://bilete.cfrcalatori.ro/
    • Trip duration, distance in km, transfer time
    • Trip data for 6th November 2022 (Sunday)
    • Selection criteria:
      • Chose InterRegio (InterRegional/IR) trains when possible
      • Least transfers possible
      • Transfers times of at least 25 minutes
      • Shortest trip duration possible, regardless of time of day
  3. https://maps.google.com
    • Most optimistic estimate
    • Trip data for 6th November 2022 (Sunday - chosen to minimize in-city traffic delays)
    • Starting point is city center

Data manipulation:

  1. Joined in Excel the following columns:
    • County, County seat, NUTS code (data source 1),
    • Trip duration train, Distance in km train, Transfer time (data source 2)
    • Trip duration car, Distance in km car
  2. Calculated fields:
    • New columns with the number of hours in a numerical format (instead of the hh:mm:ss format) for trip duration columns
    • New columns with average speeds (kmps) by train and car

Visualization:

Average train and car travel speeds from Bucharest to county seat cities

Note: transfer time was included in the calculation of the average speed. The values do not reflect the actual average speed of trains for routes that included transfers (1 transfer: Resita, Calarasi, Slobozia, Zalau, Alexandria, Tulcea)

The color scale for average speeds:

  • Minimum 40 kmph (Giurgiu 44.5 kmph by train)
  • Maximum 115 kmph (Constanta 112 kmph by car)
  • Red (slower) to green (faster)

Average train and car travel duration from Bucharest to county seat cities

The color scale for duration:

  • Minimum 0.5 hours (Ploiesti 0.55 hours)
  • Maximum 15 hours (Satu Mare 14.27 hours)
  • Green (shorter) to red (longer)
